Josh Shields

About Me:

Josh is a senior graduate student in astrophysics working primarily on the Type Ia supernova progenitor problem. He does systematic searches of supernova remnants both inside and outside our Galaxy to identify stars that were in a binary system with the white dwarf that exploded to create the remnant. He works with large scale stellar surveys like Gaia, some of the largest ground based telescopes like the VLT, and with space based data like the HST.
